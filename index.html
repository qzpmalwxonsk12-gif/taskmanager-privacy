Privacy Policy

TaskManager (com.skai.taskmanager) · Effective date: 14 July 2026

The short version: TaskManager does not collect, store, transmit, share, or sell any personal data. The app works entirely offline, does not request internet access, contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs. Everything you create in the app stays on your device.

1. Who we are

TaskManager is a personal day-planner app for Android that combines a to-do list, a focus timer, reminders, and notes. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

2. Data we collect

None. TaskManager does not collect any data — personal or otherwise. Specifically, the app has:

  • No internet permission — it is technically incapable of sending data off your device.
  • No user accounts, sign-in, or registration.
  • No advertising and no advertising ID usage.
  • No analytics, crash reporting, or tracking of any kind.
  • No third-party SDKs or services.

3. Data stored on your device

Everything you create in the app — tasks, notes, schedules, focus-session statistics, settings, and the optional profile name, bio, and photo — is stored in a private local database on your device only. It is never uploaded, synced, or backed up to any server by us, and no one (including the developer) can access it.

If you pick a custom alert sound, the app only remembers a reference to the audio file you chose so it can play it for your reminders. The file is never copied or transmitted.

4. Permissions the app uses and why

PermissionWhy it is needed
Notifications To show your task list, task-time reminders, note reminders, and the focus-session timer.
Run at startup To re-schedule your reminders after the device reboots (alarms are cleared by Android on restart).
Foreground service Used only while a focus session you started is running, so the live countdown keeps working when the app is closed. It stops when the session ends.
Vibration For the alert profiles (vibrate, chime, melody, alarm) you assign to tasks.
Full-screen notifications Optionally shows the end-of-session alert over the lock screen when a focus timer finishes.
Do Not Disturb access Optional. Only if you enable it, the app turns on Do Not Disturb during a focus session and restores your previous setting afterwards. You can revoke this at any time in system settings.

None of these permissions are used to read, collect, or transmit personal information.

5. Data sharing

We do not share data with anyone, because no data ever leaves your device. There are no third parties involved in the operation of this app.

6. Data retention and deletion

Your data exists only inside the app on your device. You can delete individual tasks and notes in the app at any time. Clearing the app's storage in Android settings, or uninstalling the app, permanently removes all of its data. There is nothing to delete on any server, because nothing is stored on one.

7. Children's privacy

TaskManager is suitable for general audiences. Since the app collects no data from any user, it also collects no data from children.

8. Changes to this policy

If a future version of the app ever changes how data is handled (for example, adding an optional backup feature), this policy will be updated before that version is released, and the effective date above will change.
